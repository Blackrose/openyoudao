reference [ 'refərəns ]

  • n.
    • a remark that calls attention to something or someone

      "the speaker made several references to his wife"

      同义词： mention

    • a short note recognizing a source of information or of a quoted passage

      同义词： citation cite acknowledgment credit mention quotation

    • an indicator that orients you generally

      "it is used as a reference for comparing the heating and the electrical energy involved"

      同义词： reference point point of reference

    • a book to which you can refer for authoritative facts

      "he contributed articles to the basic reference work on that topic"

      同义词： reference book reference work book of facts

    • a formal recommendation by a former employer to a potential future employer describing the person's qualifications and dependability

      "requests for character references are all to often answered evasively"

      同义词： character character reference

    • the most direct or specific meaning of a word or expression; the class of objects that an expression refers to

      同义词： denotation extension

    • the act of referring or consulting

      "reference to an encyclopedia produced the answer"

      同义词： consultation

    • a publication (or a passage from a publication) that is referred to

      "he carried an armful of references back to his desk"

      同义词： source

    • the relation between a word or phrase and the object or idea it refers to

      "he argued that reference is a consequence of conditioned reflexes"

  • v. refer to

    "he referenced his colleagues' work"

    同义词： cite

柯林斯高级英汉双解词典

ref·er·ence /rɛfərəns, rɛfrəns/ ( references ) CET4

  • N-VAR Reference to someone or something is the act of talking about them or mentioning them. A reference is a particular example of this. 提及

    例: He made no reference to any agreement.

    他没有提到任何协议。

  • N-UNCOUNT Reference is the act of consulting someone or something in order to get information or advice. 咨询; 查阅

    例: Please keep this sheet in a safe place for reference.

    请把这张纸放在稳妥的地方以备查阅。

  • ADJ Reference books are ones that you look at when you need specific information or facts about a subject. 供参考的 [ADJ n]

    例: ...a useful reference work for teachers.

    …一本对老师们有用处的参考资料。

  • N-COUNT A reference is a word, phrase, or idea which comes from something such as a book, poem, or play and which you use when making a point about something. 引文; 引文出处

    例: ...a reference from the Koran.

    …一段《可兰经》的引文。

  • N-COUNT A reference is something such as a number or a name that tells you where you can obtain the information you want. 编号; 标记

    例: Make a note of the reference number shown on the form.

    记下表格上的编码。

  • N-COUNT A reference is a letter that is written by someone who knows you and which describes your character and abilities. When you apply for a job, an employer might ask for references. 推荐信

    例: The firm offered to give her a reference.

    公司提出给她开一封推荐信。

  • N-COUNT A reference is a person who gives you a reference, for example when you are applying for a job. 证明人 [mainly AM]

    英国英语通常为referee

    例: The official at the American embassy asked me for two references.

    美国大使馆的官员要我提供两名证明人。

  • PHRASE You use with reference to or in reference to in order to indicate what something relates to. 关于…

    例: I am writing with reference to your article on salaries for scientists.

    我给你写信是要谈谈你写的有关科学家工资的那篇文章。

21世纪大英汉词典

reference ['refərəns]

  • n.
    • 参考，参照，参看；查阅
    • 提交；委托(指行为)
    • 提及，说到，涉及
    • 揭示，指引；引证，引文
    • 关系；关联
    • (关于某人的工作能力、品德等方面的)证明文件；介绍信，推荐信
    • (关于能力、品德等方面的)证明人；介绍人
    • 参考书；参考书目
  • vt.
    • 给(书、论文等)附加参考资料：

      Each chapter is referenced in minute detail.

      每一章后面都附有极为详尽的参考资料。

    • 附…以供参考：

      Tables of measures and weights are referenced at the end of the book.

      书末附有度量衡表以供参考。

    • 把…引作参考；引用，访问：

      A variable in a computer program references a location in memory.

      计算机程序中的变量引用存储单元。

  • 短语:
    • cross reference to 互相参阅(参看同书中的其他章节)；参见；互见
    • in reference to 关于
    • make reference (to) 参看，参考；提及，涉及，说到
    • terms of reference 责任范围；审查范围
    • without reference to 无论，不管，不顾；与…无关
    • with reference to = in reference to
    • 变形：

      vt. referenced . referencing
