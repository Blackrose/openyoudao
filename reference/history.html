re·quest /rɪkwɛst/ ( requests, requesting, requested ) CET4

  • V-T If you request something, you ask for it politely or formally. 请求; 正式要求 [FORMAL]

    例: Mr. Dennis said he had requested access to a telephone.

    丹尼斯先生说他已正式要求要能够使用一部电话。

  • V-T If you request someone to do something, you politely or formally ask them to do it. 要求 [FORMAL]

    例: Students are requested to park at the rear of the building.

    学生们被要求在那座楼后面停车。

  • N-COUNT If you make a request, you politely or formally ask someone to do something. 请求

    例: France had agreed to his request for political asylum.

    法国已经同意了他政治避难的请求。

  • PHRASE If you do something at someone's request, you do it because they have asked you to. 应某人的要求

    例: The evacuation is being organized at the request of the United Nations Secretary General.

    应联合国秘书长的要求，该撤离正在进行中。

  • PHRASE If something is given or done on request, it is given or done whenever you ask for it. 应要求

    例: Details are available on request.

    详情备索。

request [ri'kwest]

  • n.
    • 要求；请求
    • 要求的事物
    • 需要
    • 申请书
  • vt.
    • 要求，请求:

      May I request a favour of you?

      请您帮一下忙好吗?

    • 请求得到；索取：

      We request the favour of a reply at your earliest convenience.

      敬请早日赐复。

    • 邀请[常接不定式或从句]：

      to request someone to attend the opening ceremony

      邀请某人参加开幕典礼

      She requested Tom to go with her.

      她邀请汤姆一同去。

      Mr John Smith requests the pleasure of the company of Mr William White at a dinner at 7:00 pm on 21 April.

      约翰史密斯先生敬请威廉怀特先生光临4月21日下午7时的晚宴。

  • 短语:
    • at the request of someone 应某人的请求[亦作at someone's request]
    • by request (of) 应…的请求(或邀请)
    • come into request 出现(对某事物的)需要
    • comply with (或grant ) someone's request 应某人的请求
    • in (great) request (很)为大众所喜爱；(很)为人们所需要
    • make a request 要求；提出请求
    • on (或upon) request 经要求
